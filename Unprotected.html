Unprotected
(Short Film, 2017)

Unprotected is an NYU thesis film directed by Grace Giffune. It tells the story of teenage girl Shay, who finds herself pregnant and with superhero powers. Rather unwillingly she's immersed into the world of Superheroes and Supervillains as she attempts to find the father of her child. This short is currently in Post-Production.
