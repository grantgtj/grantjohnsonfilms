YoYoExpert - Offstring
(Tutorial Series, 2016)

YoYoExpert - Offstring is a web tutorial series shot for yo-yo distributor "YoYoExpert". The series consists of over 60 yo-yo tutorials in varying trick difficulties, as well as an introduction montage video that highlights the goals of the project. The series is currently in Post-Production.
